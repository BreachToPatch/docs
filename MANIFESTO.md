# BreachToPatch Manifesto

> "From shadow to light, from breach to patch."

BreachToPatch (BtoP) is a community-driven, open-source cybersecurity platform. 
Our mission is to bridge the gap between offensive exploitation and defensive 
hardening through a continuous, code-based learning cycle.

### Our Core Principles
1. **Infrastructure as Code**: No heavy images, just versionable files.
2. **Double-Edged Exploits**: Attack scripts become the regression tests for defense.
3. **Open Growth**: Every pwn and every patch contributes to the community's collective security knowledge.
